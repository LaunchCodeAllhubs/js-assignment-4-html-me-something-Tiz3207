The land of Origins: Ethiopia

Ethiopia is one of the oldest country in the world and the only sub-Saharan African country that was never colonised by European countries, maintaining its independence throughout the Scramble for Africa. The Ethiopian calendar is unique and has 13 months which makes the Ethiopian calendar 7 years behind the rest of the world. Ethiopia is known for its rich history and culture, including ancient civilizations, unique cuisine and takes first place for the African country with the most UNESCO World Heritage sites. There are 9 Ethiopian Heritage registered by UNESCO. These are Axum, Fasil Ghebbi, Harar Jugol, Awash river, Rock-Hewn monolithic churches of Lalibela, Tiya Stelae Field, Simien Mountains National park, Konso Cultural Landscape and Lower valey of the Omo River.
